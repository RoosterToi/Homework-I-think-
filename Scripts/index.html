My favorite game

I have many favorite games, but in this webpage I will be talking about one.

ROBLOX, is my favorite game, with many different platforms to play on, like Xbox, Computer, Mac, and even iPhone and iPod touch!

Cross Play

ROBLOX added a feature called Cross Play which meant that you can play ROBLOX with your friends on VR,Xbox, PC or Mac, any Mobile devices, at the same time! When this happens, this is known as using a Third Party Server.

Social Universe

In ROBLOX, you can socialize and talk to other people, customize your own character, and even send friend requests to people you like!
